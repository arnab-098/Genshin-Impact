Eula is a Cryo Claymore user in Genshin Impact. As a descendant of the disgraced Lawrence clan, she faces prejudice but has proven her worth as the Captain of the Knights of Favonius Reconnaissance Company. Known for her powerful attacks and complex personality, Eula is a formidable force on the battlefield.

Yelan is a mysterious Hydro Bow user in Genshin Impact. Working for Liyue's Ministry of Civil Affairs, her true role is shrouded in secrecy. Known for agility and intelligence, she excels as an investigator and combatant. With a playstyle focused on Hydro damage and crowd control, Yelan is a versatile addition to any team.

Xianyun is a 5-star Anemo Catalyst character in Genshin Impact. Originally an Adeptus known as Cloud Retainer, she's descended to the mortal realm to explore its wonders. As a support character, Xianyun focuses on enhancing Plunging Attacks, providing healing, and offering buffs to the team. Her playstyle revolves around strategic aerial combat, making her a unique addition to any party.

Yae Miko is a cunning and enigmatic Electro Catalyst user in Genshin Impact. As the Guuji of the Grand Narukami Shrine, she maintains a mysterious facade while secretly running the Yae Publishing House. With her playful personality and powerful Electro abilities, she's a formidable character both in combat and in manipulating situations to her advantage.

Raiden Shogun is the Electro Archon and ruler of Inazuma. Initially presented as a stern and imposing figure, the Shogun is actually a puppet created by the true Archon, Raiden Ei. Known for her Electro Polearm abilities, she excels in energy management and burst damage. Beneath the stoic exterior lies a complex character driven by a desire to protect her people and preserve eternity.

Arlecchino is one of the Eleven Fatui Harbingers, a mysterious organization that serves the Tsaritsa. Known as the "Dame of the Puppet," she is a cruel and manipulative individual who wields immense power. While her element and weapon type remain unknown, her role in the Fatui's schemes is significant and sinister.
